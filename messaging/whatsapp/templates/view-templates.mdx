title: "View Templates"
---

WhatsApp templates can be viewed and managed from either the Belio Cloud Portal or the WhatsApp Business Manager.

### Belio Cloud Portal
1. Navigate to [Belio portal](https://cloud.belio.co.ke/whatsapp-business)


---

### WhatsApp Business Manager
1. Navigate to the [WhatsApp Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com/latest/whatsapp_manager)
2. Sign in with your Facebook Business account
3. Select your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)
4. Open the **Message Templates** section

The Message Templates page allows you to:
- View all approved, pending, and rejected templates
- Filter templates by category or status
- Inspect template structure and placeholders
- Review rejection reasons for failed submissions

---

### Template Statuses

Templates may appear in different states depending on their review and approval status.

| Status     | Description                                                          |
|------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `Pending`  | Template is under review by WhatsApp                                 |
| `Approved` | Template has been approved and can be used for sending messages      |
| `Rejected` | Template was rejected and cannot be used                             |
| `Disabled` | Template has been disabled by WhatsApp                               |
| `Paused`   | Template has been temporarily paused due to policy or quality issues |

---

#### Important Notes

<Tip>
    Only templates in an approved and sendable state can be used with the WhatsApp Messaging API.<br/>

    Template availability and statuses are synchronized from the connected WhatsApp Business Account (WABA).
</Tip>
