---
title: "Create Template"
---

Templates can be created using either of the following platforms:

1. **[Belio Cloud Portal](https://cloud.belio.co.ke/)**
    - Create and manage WhatsApp templates directly from the Belio platform.

2. **[WhatsApp Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com/latest/whatsapp_manager)**
    - Create templates through Meta's WhatsApp Business Manager.
    - Requires an active Facebook Business account
    - Templates submitted through Meta must still comply with WhatsApp policies and approval requirements
